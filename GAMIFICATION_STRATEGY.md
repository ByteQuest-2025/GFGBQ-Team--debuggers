## ğŸ’¡ Key Design Principles

### 1. **Offline-First**
- All learning content downloadable
- Progress syncs when online
- Works on 2G networks

### 2. **Lightweight**
- Each module < 2MB
- Minimal animations (saves battery)
- Works on low-end phones

### 3. **Jargon-Free**
- "Your money grows" not "Compound interest"
- "Safe investment" not "Debt fund"
- Simple language, local examples

### 4. **Fear Reduction**
- Start with virtual money
- Small amounts (â‚¹10 minimum)
- Clear risk indicators
- No pressure to invest

### 5. **Trust Building**
- Transparent about fees (zero or minimal)
- Show real examples from similar users
- Clear, simple terms
- No hidden charges

### 6. **Progressive Disclosure**
- Don't overwhelm with information
- Unlock features gradually
- "You'll learn this in Module 5"

## ğŸ¨ UI/UX Considerations

### Visual Design:
- **Minimal, Clean**: Warm white & black theme (already set)
- **Icons Over Text**: More visual, less reading
- **Large Touch Targets**: Easy to tap on small screens

- **Swipe to Learn**: Swipe through lessons (familiar gesture)

- **Pull to Refresh**: Check portfolio updates
- **Haptic Feedback**: Subtle vibration on achievements

### Accessibility:
- **Large Text Option**: For users with vision issues
- **Voice Narration**: Read lessons aloud (optional)
- **Simple Navigation**: Max 3 taps to any feature
- **Error Prevention**: Confirm before big actions
